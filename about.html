A Charleston Tradition

Our Story

A neighborhood donut shop serving Charleston for decades — and still going.

There are some places that become part of a community’s story.

For decades, Donut Connection Charleston has been one of them. Generations of families have made their way through our doors — before school, before work, on Saturday mornings, and on the days worth celebrating. This is the story of how we got here, and the tradition Theresa Ortiz is proud to continue today.

Continuing the Tradition

Meet Theresa

Donut Connection Charleston is locally owned by Theresa Ortiz, who is proud to carry forward a tradition that started long before her — serving the Charleston community with fresh donuts, coffee, cakes, and treats for everyday mornings and special celebrations.

Theresa’s goal is simple: keep the quality, friendliness, and familiarity that made this shop a Charleston staple in the first place, while making sure every customer still feels like family.

Whether you’re stopping in for coffee before work, picking up donuts for the office, ordering cupcakes for a birthday, or planning treats for a special event, Donut Connection Charleston is here to make the day a little sweeter — just like it always has been.
