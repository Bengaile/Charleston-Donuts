About Theresa

The story behind Charleston, WV’s sweetest stop.

Meet Theresa Ortiz

Donut Connection Charleston is locally owned by Theresa Ortiz, who is proud to serve the Charleston community with fresh donuts, coffee, cakes, and treats for everyday mornings and special celebrations.

Theresa’s goal is simple: provide quality products, friendly service, and a welcoming place where customers feel like family.

Whether customers are stopping in for coffee before work, picking up donuts for the office, ordering cupcakes for a birthday, or planning treats for a special event, Donut Connection Charleston is here to make the day a little sweeter.
